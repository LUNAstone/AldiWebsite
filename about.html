Did you know?

  • Aldi was first created in Germany by Karl and Theo Albrecht in 1946. They aimed to create a supermarket where people who didn't have much money would be able to get good groceries for much cheaper than the larger supermarkets.
  • The name 'Aldi' comes from the German full name: "Albrecht Diskont"
  • Aldi is actually two different companies across the whole world. There are Aldi Nord (North) and Aldi Süd (South). Aldi UK is a part of Aldi Süd!

Our Dedication to Our Suppliers

We're committed to sourcing our products from the UK, championing Great British quality is one of our main goals.

All meat and milk is British-made and from Red Tractor approved farms. And that isn't just an overblown clai, 94% of our customers in a 1000 person sample believed that we adhered to the grocery code of practice standard across Britain.

We also have strong ties with the Rainforest Alliance and Fairtrade, selling a range of certified products across Great Britain amassed for over twelve million pounds worth of sales in Britain and Ireland!
